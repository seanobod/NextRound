A simple diagnosis example

The value is not just rewriting words. It is identifying why the original version feels generic, then turning it into something more specific and role-focused.

Original issue

“Hard-working graduate with excellent communication skills seeking an opportunity to develop professionally.”


This sounds positive, but it is too broad. It does not show what the applicant has done, what role they are targeting, or why they match the job description.

Improved version

“Business graduate with customer-facing experience and university project work in market research, campaign planning, and content creation. Comfortable analysing customer needs, writing clear communications, and supporting collaborative teams.”


This version is stronger because it gives the employer concrete experience to connect to the role.

FAQ

Why not just use ChatGPT myself?

You can, and for some people that may be enough. The problem is that many AI-generated applications still sound generic because the hard part is not generating words. The hard part is knowing what the job description is really asking for, what parts of your experience are relevant, what should be removed, and how to keep everything truthful and human-sounding. NextRound uses AI to speed up analysis and drafting, but the value is in the diagnosis, tailoring, and manual review.

Do you guarantee I will get an interview?

No. No CV or application service can honestly guarantee interviews. The goal is to make your application clearer, more relevant, and better matched to the role.

Will you add achievements or experience I do not have?

No. The application will be improved, but it must stay truthful. I can help frame your real experience better, but I will not invent experience.

AI is used to analyse the job description, identify useful keywords, generate drafts, and improve structure. The final application pack is manually reviewed before delivery.
